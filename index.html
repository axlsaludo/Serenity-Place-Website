Frequently Asked Questions

Got Some Questions, Here are some of the most common ones

  • How do I make a reservation?

    You can book your stay directly through our website by selecting your desired dates and room type, filling in your details, and confirming your booking with a payment.

  • Can I change or cancel my reservation?

    Yes, you can change or cancel your reservation through our website. Please refer to our cancellation policy for details about deadlines and any applicable fees.

  • What types of rooms are available?

    We offer a variety of room types, including standard rooms, KTV, and large accommodations. Each room type is described in detail on our booking page.

  • Do you offer any special rates or discounts?

    We offer seasonal promotions, group discounts, and special rates for extended stays.

  • Are pets allowed in the hotel?

    Yes you paw childrens are welcome here. Please mention your pet when booking and check our pet policy regulations.
